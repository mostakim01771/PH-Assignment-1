Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before registering.

What's included in my ticket?

All tickets include access to the full 3 days of talks, networking lunches, and coffee breaks. Workshop access, recordings, and extras depend on your tier — see the pricing table above for the full breakdown.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes — full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague free of charge at any time, even on the day itself.

Will the talks be recorded?

Yes. Standard ticket holders get access to recordings for 30 days after the conference. Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access to all session recordings.

Is there a remote / virtual attendance option?

DevConf 2026 is an in-person event, but keynotes will be live-streamed for free. Recordings of all other sessions are available through ticket tiers.

Do you offer student or group discounts?

Students get 40% off Standard tickets with a valid student ID. For groups of 10+, the Team tier is the best value — or contact us for larger custom packages.

How do I become a sponsor or speaker?

We'd love to hear from you. Speaker applications and sponsorship inquiries are open until March 2026 — reach out at hello@devconf2026.com.
